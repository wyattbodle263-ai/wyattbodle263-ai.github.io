# Privacy Policy for CutList Planner

_Last updated: 7/13/2026

CutList Planner ("the app") is developed by Wyatt Bodle ("we", "us"). This
policy explains what data the app handles.

## Summary

CutList Planner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The
app has no account system, no cloud sync, and no analytics or advertising
SDKs. Everything you enter — projects, cut lists, lumber inventory — is
stored only in local storage on your device and is never sent anywhere.

## Data we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos,
device identifiers, or usage analytics. We do not use cookies, trackers, or
third-party advertising networks.

## In-app purchases

Subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play's
billing system. We never see or store your payment information. Purchase
status is verified locally on your device using the platform's standard
in-app purchase APIs; we do not run a server and do not receive any purchase
or billing data ourselves.

## Data you enter

Project names, cut-list dimensions, and lumber inventory you type into the
app are stored locally in the app's private storage on your device. This
data stays on your device. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted.
We have no access to it at any time.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any
information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect
information from anyone at all.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes (for example, if a future version adds an optional
cloud backup feature), we will update this page and change the "Last
updated" date above.
